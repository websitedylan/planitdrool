About

PLANIT DROOL (DROOL VS) — artist, songwriter, and mix-engineer.

I blend hook-heavy dark-pop with rock-rap energy and Baton Rouge grit, building the villainverse under Broken Records Entertainment.

Influences: Weezer, Red Hot Chili Peppers, Hollywood Undead, Eminem, Lil Wayne. Based in Los Angeles; currently studying audio engineering at LAFS and releasing two projects a year with singles in between.

  • Home studio: Ableton Live 12, Scarlett 2i2, AT mic & headphones, Auto-Tune, Splice
  • Services: writing, recording, vocal production, mixing
  • Highlights: Open-mic host, “Skeleton in My Closet”, Phoneheadz trilogy
